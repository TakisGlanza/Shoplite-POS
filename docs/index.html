Shoplite POS

Simple Windows POS & inventory manager for small shops – with a 5-day free trial.

Shoplite POS is a lightweight point of sale and inventory management application that runs locally on your Windows PC using a fast SQLite database. It is designed for small stores that want a simple, offline tool to track products, stock levels, suppliers, purchase orders and daily sales.

Download Shoplite POS

Key features

  • Local Windows POS and inventory manager, no cloud or subscription
  • Products with barcode, category, supplier, stock, cost and retail prices
  • Quick stock in/out with automatic transaction logging
  • Low stock list so you never run out of important items
  • Supplier management and purchase order generation
  • Receiving purchase orders automatically updates stock and costs
  • Integrated POS sales screen suitable for barcode scanners
  • CSV export for products, low stock and purchase orders
  • Basic analytics: top products, sales by category, daily sales and profit estimates

Trial & license

Shoplite POS includes a fully functional 5-day trial. After the trial period expires, the application requires a valid license key to continue. License keys are activated offline, and all data remains stored locally on your PC.

System requirements

  • Windows 10 or later (64-bit recommended)
  • At least 2 GB RAM (4 GB recommended)
  • About 200 MB of free disk space
  • Internet connection only needed to download the installer
